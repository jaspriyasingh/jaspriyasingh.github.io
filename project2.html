KVC Website
Redesign

A website is a medium to communicate a brand visually!

OUTLINE OF THE PROJECT

  • Focus on increasing sales, building brand awareness, and improving customer relationships.
  • Enhancing the business reputation.
  • Converting website traffic into sales.

    • TARGET AUDIENCE

  • People looking to travel to Hawaii for a holiday
  • Newly weds going for their honeymoon to Hawaii
  • A small family of 3-5 people/group of 3-5 friends

    • PROBLEMS / PAIN POINTS OF THE PREVIOUS WEBSITE

  • The design of the website is not consistent with the content.
  • The visitors of the site are not visually attracted towards the design.
  • The images open in a new window which distracts the customer.
  • The website looks old and needs to feel like that of the present era.

    • SOLUTIONS TO IMPROVE THE WEBSITE

  • HOMEPAGE - Creating the first impression that lasts forever!
  • LOGO/BRANDING - Adding a logo to help customers perceive the brand.
  • TYPOGRAPHY - Using at the most two fonts (sans-serif)
  • COLOR - Changing background color to white for readability.
  • COLOR PALETTE - Using gradients and cool colors to represent Hawaii.
  • LAYOUT - Creating Visual Hierarchy and using a consistent theme.
  • NAVIGATION - A well-constructed navigation can help visitors to have a very positive experience.
  • USABILITY - Using lists/drop down menus, adding titles to each webpage.
  • MULTIMEDIA - Adding virtual tour of the property, images, video, graphics.
