Namrta Birmi


BollywoodMoves, founded in 2011, by Namrta Birmi, is a dance & fitness company based in Singapore, which specializes in corporate dance events and team building workshops. BollywoodMoves also provides dance fitness packages with a unique edge of bollywood. The company also offers a variety of dance classes like Bollywood Choreography, Bolly Fitness, Zumba, Zumbatomic (Zumba for kids), Piloxing and Bokwa.

Namrta Birmi is a certified AAFA group fitness instructor. She has been in the dance fitness business in Singapore for over 7 years. She is the Bokwa ambassador for Singapore, and is also a certified Zumba & Bollywood dance instructor who has choreographed for corporate events, flashmobs, sports fitness concerts, charity events, fashion shows, and other social and cultural events in Singapore. She strives to build each student's self-esteem to rock the stage at any event or social gathering. Her goal is to get everyone involved and inspire them to dance for fun and fitness.

BollywoodMoves has conducted special bollywood workshops for company sports day/family day events for some of the big corporates in Singapore like Fedex, DHL, Citibank, GE Energy, Charles & Keith, Jetstar Asia, UIC Ltd, MPG Asia, Bank of New York Mellon, Marina Bay Sands, Singapore Army, MEC Global, Eu Yan Sang and it continues to perform and be a part of a variety of events.
